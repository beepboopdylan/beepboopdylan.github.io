O city, a bitter lament set up as thy lament;
Thy lament which is bitter—
O city, set up as thy lament,
His righteous city which has been destroyed—
Bitter is its lament;
His Ur which has been destroyed—
Bitter is its lament; Thy lament which is bitter—
O city, set up as thy lament;
His Ur which has been destroyed—
Bitter is its lament.
- Lament for Ur, c. 2000 BC
